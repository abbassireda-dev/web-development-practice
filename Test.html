About Me

Morocco:

Location: Morocco is located in North Africa, bordered by the Atlantic Ocean and the Mediterranean Sea.
Capital: Rabat
Language: Arabic is the official language, but French is also widely spoken.
Culture: Morocco has a rich cultural heritage, known for its vibrant markets (souks), historic cities like Marrakech and Fes, and diverse cuisine including couscous, tagine, and mint tea.
Tourist Attractions: Popular tourist destinations in Morocco include the ancient city of Marrakech, the blue city of Chefchaouen, the Sahara Desert, and the coastal city of Casablanca.

Gannan Normal University, Ganzhou:

Location: Gannan Normal University is located in Ganzhou, Jiangxi Province, China.
Overview: Gannan Normal University is a comprehensive university offering undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various fields including education, literature, science, engineering, and management.
Campus Life: The university provides a vibrant campus life with various student organizations, cultural events, and sports activities.
International Students: Gannan Normal University welcomes students from various countries, fostering cultural exchange and cooperation.
