Gannan Normal University

Gannan Normal University is a comprehensive university located in Ganzhou, Jiangxi Province, China. Founded in 1941, the university has a rich history and a strong commitment to providing high-quality education.

With its picturesque campus, modern facilities, and dedicated faculty, Gannan Normal University offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in fields such as education, literature, science, engineering, and management.

At Gannan Normal University, students have access to a vibrant campus life, including various student organizations, cultural events, and sports activities. The university also welcomes international students, fostering cultural exchange and cooperation.
